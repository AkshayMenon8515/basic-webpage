ABOUT

Globally, we are a leading international banking group, with a presence in 53 of the world's most dynamic markets. Our purpose is to drive commerce and prosperity through our unique diversity, and our heritage and values are expressed in our brand promise, here for good

Click here to visit our website

Standard Chartered Bank has been operating in Bahrain since 1920, making it the first bank to establish a presence in the country. It has played a significant role in developing Bahrain into a key financial center. The bank employs 532 staff and maintains seven branches across the country, boasting the most extensive branch network among international banks in Bahrain.
